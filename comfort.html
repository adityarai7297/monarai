Comfort Care

This service aims at providing continued care to patients who have been discharged from the hospital after a major surgery or an acute medical condition and need extended medical support in their homes in the form of Nurses, Doctor visit, continued monitoring or medical equipments.

My team and I will facilitate the needed care in the homes of such patients, and provide the necessary medical support in liaison with the treating doctor. We will ensure compliance to the advised treatment.

  • Home visits by Doctor
  • Wound dressing
  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation
  • Mobility equipment
  • Monitoring devices on rent
  • Insertion/ Removal of Catheter
  • IV Fluids & Injections
  • Home visits by Nurses
  • Blood investigations
