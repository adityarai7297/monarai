Pain & Palliative Care

Pain is one of the most distressing symptom in cancer and various other chronic illnesses, and takes a very heavy toll on the patient and their families. My primary aim is to alleviate the pain and suffering arising out of such illnesses and provide a holistic care plan to the seriously ill patients and their families by addressing the physical, emotional and spiritual aspects of their pain.

We are a team of competent health care professionals, who understand the situation of each patient and based on his/her preferences, make care plans best suited for them. This approach aims to provide symptom care to people who have been diagnosed with life threatening illnesses. The services include personalised pain management, symptom control, nursing care, counselling and many more including :

  • Home Visits by Doctor
  • Home Visits by Nurses
  • Patient Care Assistants
  • Wound Dressing
  • Daily Hygiene Care Staff
  • Physiotherapy
  • Counsellor
  • Blood investigations
  • Teleconsultation
  • Catheterisation
  • Feeding Tubes
  • IV Fluids & Injections
