There is an end to cure
but there is no end to care

With this as our mission statement, we aim to provide expert and personalised healthcare for patients suffering from life limiting illnesses or those struggling with the countless problems arising out of old age. The only focus of my team is to improve the quality of life of our patients by addressing the physical, emotional and spiritual aspects of their health needs within the comfort of their own homes.
I come up with personalised healthcare plans for each individual according to his/her needs, along the entire trajectory of their illness. We cater to patients and their family who are terminally ill, need health/comfort care facilities for senior citizens, or to cope with the burden of any other health related suffering that requires continued care.

Services

Pain & Palliative care

Pain is one of the most distressing symptoms in cancer and various other chronic illnesses, and takes a very heavy toll on the patient and their family. My primary aim is to alleviate the pain and suffering arising out of such illnesses and provide a holistic care plan to the seriously ill patients and their family by addressing the physical, emotional and spiritual aspects of their pain.

Read More

Elderly Care

There is a myth which says that old age inevitably means compromising on one’s quality of life. This service plans to break that myth and provide a structured and comprehensive health care plan for the elderly that will take care of their health needs and well-being, in the comfort of their own homes.

Read More

Comfort Care

This service aims at providing continued care to patients who have been discharged from the hospital after a major surgery or an acute medical condition and need extended medical support in their homes in the form of Nurses, Doctor visit, continued monitoring or medical equipment.

About

The "Mona Rai" Assurance

In the field of care there are no standardisations. Which is why I personally formulate the health care strategy for each patient myself, in consultation with experts from required fields. This personalisation ensures that you and your loved ones receive the best health care support at all times. In my journey as a doctor I am continually moved by serious health-related suffering. Which is why I am committed to providing care to general patients and elderly in the comfort of their homes. My specialisation in Pain & Palliative Care and Geriatric Medicine helps me bridge the gap between cure and care, and provide a continuum of health care to people diagnosed with serious illnesses. I have a team of trained, experienced, competent and polite professionals on board with me to support the patients and their family struggling with serious health related issues.
