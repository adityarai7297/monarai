There is an end to cure
but there is no end to care

With this as our mission statement, we aims to provide expert and personalised healthcare for patients suffering from life limiting illnesses or those struggling with the countless problems arising out of old age. The only focus of my team is to improve the quality of life of our patients by addressing the physical, emotional and spiritual aspects of their health needs within the comfort of their own homes.
I personally come up with personalised healthcare plans for each individual according to his/her needs, along the entire trajectory of their illness. We cater to patients and their families who are terminally ill, need health/comfort care facilities for senior citizens, or to cope with the burden of any other health related suffering that requires continued care.

Services

Pain & Palliative care

Pain is one of the most distressing symptom in cancer and various other chronic illnesses, and takes a very heavy toll on the patient and their families. My primary aim is to alleviate the pain and suffering arising out of such illnesses and provide a holistic care plan to the seriously ill patients and their families by addressing the physical, emotional and spiritual aspects of their pain.

Elderly Care

There is a popular myth that old age inevitably means compromising on one’s quality of life. This service plans to break that myth and provide a structured and comprehensive health care plan for the elderly population that will take care of their health needs and well-being in the comfort of their own homes.

Comfort Care

This service aims at providing continued care to patients who have been discharged from the hospital after a major surgery or an acute medical condition and need extended medical support in their homes in the form of Nurses, Doctor visit, continued monitoring or medical equipment.

About

The "Mona Rai" Assurace

In the field of care there are no standardisations. Which is why I personally formulate the health care strategy for each patient myself, in consultation with experts from required fields. This personalisation ensures that you and your loved ones receive the best health care support at all times. In my journey as a doctor I was moved by the serious health related sufferings. Which is why I am committed to providing care to general patients and elderly in the comfort of their homes. My specialisation in Pain, Palliative Care and Geriatric Medicine helps me bridge the gap between cure and care, and provide a continuum of health care to people diagnosed with serious illnesses. I have a team of trained, experienced, competent and polite professionals on board with me to support the patients and their families struggling with serious health related issues.

Pain & Palliative Care

We are a team of competent health care professionals, who understand the situation of each patient and based on his/her preferences, make care plans best suited for them. This approach that aims to provide symptom care to people who have been diagnosed with life threatening illnesses. The services include personalised pain management, symptom control, nursing care, counselling and many more including :

  • Home visits by Doctor
  • Home visits by Nurse
  • Patient assistant
  • Wound dressing
  • Daily hygiene care staff
  • Physiotherapy
  • Counsellor
  • Teleconsultation
  • Catheterisation
  • Feeding tubes
Elderly Care

A team of qualified, experienced and dedicated specialists from various faculties will chart out personalised health care strategies focussed at improving the quality of lives of our beloved senior population. We will also aim to cut out risk factors involved in personal visits to clinics during this pandemic, our services will ensure the maximum care with minimum exposure.
Some of the most common services every patient receives are :

  • Home visits by Doctor
  • Health check-up package
  • Dietary advice
  • Patient assistant need based/ Nurses
  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation
  • Telemedicine
  • Elderly Immunisation
  • Ambulance services
  • Appointment with specialist
  • Equipments
  • Monthly medicine delivery
Comfort Care

My team and I will help set up that care in the homes of such patients and provide the necessary medical support in liaison with the treating doctor. We will ensure compliance to the advised treatment.

  • Home visits by Doctor
  • Wound dressing
  • Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation
  • Mobility equipment
  • Monitoring devices on rent
  • Insertion/ Removal of Catheter
  • IV Fluids
  • Home visits by Nurses
  • Blood investigations
  • Patient care product
  • Medicine delivery
  • Equipment on rent
