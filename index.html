Services

Pain & Palliative care

Managing pain arising out of chronic illnesses such as Cancer, Kidney Failure, Paralysis, Brain disease etc. takes a very heavy toll on the patient and more so on the support system that is the family. Our organisation, and me, especially, aim to provide counselling via empathy, support and open communication that always leads to better acceptance, understanding and management of the disease and its possible outcomes. Our competent, certified, holistic care has helped families deal with the problems of their loved ones, as our testimonials will tell you. Services include counselling, mental health management, nursing and guidance personalised to each and every patient or family, since it cannot be generalised.

Elderly Care

This service is aimed at elders who live on their own. A team of qualified, experienced and dedicated specialists from various faculties will chart out personalised health care strategies in the comfort of their homes. Aiming to cut out all risk factors including personal visits to clinics in this pandemic, our services will ensure the maximum care with minimum exposure.

Comfort Care

This is aimed at providing medical, nursing and associated services to patients post surgery at hospital or residence. A team of competent medical professionals monitor all daily requirements from food intake to medication to physiotherapy if required in consultation with the treating Doctor. A team of polite, trained staff will take care of associated requirements like insertion/removal of catheter, dressing, removal of stitches, insertion of feeding tubes etc. These services are a burden on family care givers as they are neither qualified nor competent at providing them at most times.

About

A little about me...

I cater to terminally ill patients and their families, bringing everyone on a common platform to accept the realities by coming to terms with the disease, its acceptance and its management. I also cater to patients requiring post-surgery recovery. A dedicated team of specialists empanelled with my organisation can provide the expertise and services required to cater to all kinds of ailments. To understand how we care, please read further.
